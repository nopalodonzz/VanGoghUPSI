About the Exhibition

Immersive Exhibition: Van Gogh @ UPSI 2026 is a digital art exhibition that invites visitors to immerse themselves in the world of imagination and emotional expression of Vincent Van Gogh’s masterpieces through an immersive experience that combines art, technology, and multimedia. The exhibition features interactive visual interpretations inspired by Van Gogh’s iconic works, offering visitors a new, profound, and captivating experience.
